Privacy Policy

Left Bank Canoe Hire respect your rights to privacy and believe it is important that you know how we handle your personal details and conduct business.


How to Contact Us

Identity Theft Precautions

We use PayPal for all credit and debit card processing – your bank & credit / debit card details are not stored or held by Left Bank Canoe Hire.
All paper documents holding personal details that are not required to be held by law, or for accounting / tax purposes, are destroyed when your order has been completed and successfully delivered to you.


Computer Security

Our web site does not automatically record e-mail addresses or other personal information, unless it is provided by you when you fill out a form on our web site.
However (in common with almost every other web site on the internet) our web servers automatically recognise technical items like IP addresses and session details. These items are used solely by our systems administrators to monitor the performance of our web service and to plan for and develop improvements to our on-line systems.


Cookies

In using our website, you are agreeing to our Terms & Conditions and Privacy Policy. Our Privacy Policy can tell you more on how we collect and use personal data.


In Summary

We use some cookies on our website. These cookies allow us to identify the computer or device you’re using to access our websites, allow us to see which pages are visited and how you move through the site – but we cannot identify you personally.
Cookies are small text files placed on your computer by us or our partners. They transmit information back to us.
Using the settings in your web browser, you can refuse cookies, but it may hamper your full use of our website.


Our Cookies

We use cookies for collecting information. They help us to learn more about how you use our website and what interest you. The data we collect from cookies is really important for us to improve your experience and improve our products and packages.

We use cookies to find out more about how you use our website. We use this information to:

  • Make it possible for you to use our website
  • Show you information and offers that are relevant to you
  • Find out more about how our customers use the site
  • If you’ve searched on our site, then left the website and then you come back later, the search panel will keep the information you provided before, so you won’t need to fill everything in again.
  • To record, measure and analyse how our customers use our website. We use this information to understand what you want from our website and which activities are proving most popular.
  • To see how you move around our website, so we can look into any errors stopping you from being able to do what you want.
  • To view which activities appeal to the same people to enable us to build relevant multi-activity packages.

Types of Cookies

Here are the types of cookies and details about what they're used for:

  • Application Cookies – for searching, mailing and buying through a website. We do use these
  • Analytical Cookies – monitor traffic to a website and how users interact with it. We do use these
  • Behavioural Cookies – generate content most relevant to your previous usage. We do not use these
  • Marketing Cookies – allow interaction with you once you’ve left a website, eg. showing adverts. We do not use these

Refusing cookies

Cookies are unique to the web browser you’re using – so if you’re using a desktop computer as well as a mobile, different data will be collected for each. Your web browser is what you use to view web pages, eg. Google Chrome, Mozilla Firefox, Microsoft Internet Explorer.

You can set your browser to accept all cookies, reject 3rd Party Cookies (from partners of the sites you visit) or reject all cookies. If you don’t allow cookies, some features of our website may not work.

By changing your browser settings, you can choose to accept or refuse cookies, pick which cookies you want or don’t want, or ask to be notified when a cookie is set. Try using the help feature in your browser to see how or searching online.

If you change your mind after accepting cookies, you can use your browser to clear any cookies which have already been set. You will need to set the browser to refuse cookies being added in the future.
