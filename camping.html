Campsites in and around Herefordshire


Camping at Whitney Bridge, The camping area is right on the river so – pull the canoe up and relax. Well maybe not – pitch the tent – light the fire and cook your supper. If you don’t feel like cooking however book a BBQ or breakfast ahead of time and be pampered
Contact 01497 831 669


The Pound Campsite, Whitney-on-Wye - Nestled on the banks of the River Wye, the site has a newly built amenities block which includes power showers, washing facilities and toilets. Breakfast is available (to order) at The Pound if you are too relaxed to prepare your own. Nearby shop and an inn for lunch and evening meals.
Contact 01497 831391


Borders Hideaway Holiday Home Park, Clyro - Borders Hideaway provides a haven of peace and tranquil relaxation with panoramic views. The park accepts Tents, Caravans, Campervans & Motorhomes. Situated above the sleepy village of Clyro, on the Welsh side of the meandering River Wye, near to Hay-on-Wye.
Contact 01497 831 485S


Penlan Campsie, nr Hay-on-Wye - 500 yards from the border between Herefordshire and Powys, this campsite offers beautiful views across the Brecon Beacons. Based on a national trust farm in an area of outstanding natural beauty, you will find a warm welcome awaits you. The site accepts touring caravans and tents. Contact 01497 831 485


New Court Farm Campsite, Preston on Wye - situated just below Monington Falls. This is a rustic campsite that allows groups to have campfires and provides packs of wood for the occasion. There is a toilet but no showers, making this ideal for groups looking for a wild setting with lots of wildlife around.
Contact 001981 500349


Ridge Hill, nr. Hereford - Located just 3 miles from the historic city of Hereford and within easy reach of local amenities the Ridgehill Caravan and Camping site affords basic facilities and is the ideal base for those wanting to explore the River Wye or see more of Herefordshire, South or Mid Wales.
Contact 01432 351293


Tresseck Campsite, Hoarwithy - Located in the picturesque village of Hoarwithy, this site is very family oriented. It is perfect if you want to explore Herefordshire, land or launch your canoe, go fishing or just relax in the peaceful riverside location. This site is for tents only.
Contact 01432 840235
