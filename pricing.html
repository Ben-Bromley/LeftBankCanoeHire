Canoe and Kayak hire - Terms & Conditions

  • Whilst on the river, participants must wear a buoyancy aid at all times
  • Children who are 12 and under need to be accompanied by an adult (1 adult per child).
  • Children who are 13 (but younger than 18) don't require the same ratio of adult:child supervision however experience level and weather conditions are all taken into consideration on a case to case basis.
  • Participants should always be considerate to other river users, for example, large tourist boats, other canoeists and fishermen.
  • Participants should only land at designated landing areas.
  • Do not leave litter on or around the river
  • Take care of equipment. We will have to seek payment for any lost or broken equipment. This does not include by fair wear and tear.
  • Any participant who appear to be under the influence of drink or drugs will not be allowed to use our boats and the final decision is ours.
