Left Bank Canoe Hire

Hire a Canoe Today

Left Bank Canoe Hire provides Canoes & Kayak hire across all stretches of the beautiful River Wye, in Hereford, Wales.

Canoe & Kayak hire starts from £20 per person, transport included and kids under 13 go half price!

The River Wye is perfect for the canoe hire, kayak hire, camping trips we offer.

We also have instructors for school groups or individuals that would like training and river guides for groups that would like us to manage their whole canoe / kayak experience.
